# Chessboard

A chessboard has 64 squares, laid out in a grid of 8 x 8.
The squares alternate between light and dark.
Ths bottom right-hand corner should be a light square for both players, whether you are playing with an actual boardor online.
Horizontal lines on the chessboard are ranks, the bottom of chessboard is considered as 1st rank, etc.
Vertical lines on the board are files, the leftmost of chessboard is a file, etc.
And there are diagonals going in slope way.

# Chess

The chesses are dividied into light and dark.

# Chesses

## Types of chesses

1. Rooks
   There are two rooks.

2. Knights
   There are two knights.

3. Bishops
   There are two bishops

4. Queen and King
   They are placed in the middle of the bottom row.
   Queen is the one having regular crown, while King has a cross on it crown.
   Physically King is taller then the Queen.

5. Pawns
   There are eight pawns, filling up the second bottom row.

## Starting Position of the Chesses

1. Ensure the bottom right hand corner is light square.
2. Set up Rooks, Knights and Bishops first.
3. The Queen and the King are on the centre.
   When the player is light, the Queen is on the light box as starting position, and vice versa.
4. Set up the Pawns.
